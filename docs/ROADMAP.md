## Success Metrics

We measure our success by:

- **User Satisfaction**: Net Promoter Score (NPS) > 50
- **Creative Output**: Stories completed using the platform
- **Community Growth**: Active users and contributors
- **Feature Adoption**: Usage of AI-enhanced features
- **Quality**: Bug reports and resolution time
- **Impact**: Published works that used our platform

## Guiding Principles

1. **User First**: Every feature must serve the writer
2. **Quality Over Speed**: Better to do it right than do it fast
3. **Privacy Matters**: Protect creative work at all costs
4. **Accessible**: Keep it usable for all skill levels
5. **Open**: Transparent development and community input
6. **Ethical AI**: Responsible and fair use of AI technology
